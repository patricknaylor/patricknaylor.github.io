Research

I study geometric and low dimensional topology in dimensions 3 and 4. Questions about 4-manifolds are particularly interesting because of exotic phenomena: objects which are topologically the same but smoothly distinct. Most of my work has been constructive, i.e., about producing diffeomorphisms, or proving that certain objects are smoothly equivalent.

I also study trisections (a decomposition of a smooth 4-manifold into three handlebodies), which are a bit like Heegaard splittings, and in particular may be described by diagrams of curves on surfaces.
