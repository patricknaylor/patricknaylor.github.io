Research

More about me: I study geometric and low dimensional topology, mostly in dimensions 3 and 4. Questions about 4-manifolds are particularly interesting due to the possibility of exotic phenomena: objects which are topologically the same but smoothly distinct. I'm interested in knotted surfaces in 4-manifolds; concordances/isotopy between them, as well as using them to do surgery. Most of my work has been constructive, i.e., about producing diffeomorphisms, or proving that certain objects are smoothly equivalent.

I also study trisections (a decomposition of a smooth 4-manifold into 1-handlebody pieces), which are a bit like Heegaard splittings for 4-manifolds, and in particular may be described by diagrams of curves on surfaces.
